About Section

About Me

My name is Tshering Choden, and I come from Kago, Athang under Wangduephodrang. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s of Education in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) at Samtse College of Education. Through this program, I am working to develop both technical expertise and pedagogical skills that will prepare me to contribute meaningfully as an ICT educator in the future.

My academic journey has strengthened my ability to adapt to new technologies and to approach challenges with problem-solving and critical thinking. I am committed to continuous learning and growth, with the aim of equipping myself to guide and inspire students in an increasingly digital world. Education, to me, is not just about teaching technical knowledge but also about empowering learners to think creatively and apply ICT in ways that positively impact their lives and communities.

Beyond academics, I value balance and creativity, often exploring stories and perspectives through dramas and films during my free time. These interests broaden my understanding of human experiences and remind me of the importance of empathy and communication in teaching. Driven by determination and purpose, I strive to embrace each opportunity as part of my professional and personal journey.
